Cold Soba

Description

Cold soba for a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of dried soba noodles
  • 1 cup of prepared dashi stock
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of mirin
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
    • <1i>1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 sheet nori, cut into thin strips

Steps

  1. Bring a lightly salted pot of water to a boil. Add soba noodles; cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain. Rinse with cold water to speed up cooling process
  2. Combine dash, soy sauce, mirin, and white sugar in a small saucepan; bring to a boil. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature, about 25 minutes.
  3. Toss noodles with sesame seeds and divide among 4 serving bowls. Spoon dash sauce over noodles. Top with green onions and nori.
